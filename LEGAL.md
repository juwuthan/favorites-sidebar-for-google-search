## New-Tab Disclosure

The extension changes the Microsoft Edge new-tab page using the standard browser extension new-tab override API. New tabs are redirected to `https://www.google.com/`.

Users can reverse this behavior at any time by disabling or removing the extension from `edge://extensions`.

## Bookmark and Page Disclosure

The extension reads the user's Edge favorites bar locally and renders bookmark titles and URLs into the Google Search page while the sidebar or launcher is visible.

The extension does not operate a server, include analytics, sell data, or track users across sites. See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).

## Third-Party Pages
